About US

Fundabird is committed to turning ideas into success.

At Fundabird, we target crowdfunding as a major selling point for more than just a product but combining the efforts of the entire industry chain, including the market leaders, consumers, real cash flow and promotions, and more...

We aim to support both business owners, project creators, and consumers. We strive to link audiences and businesses together. We provide the market platform and business revenue through traffic while offering consumers low-priced and high-quality services and products. At the same time, members can also participate in crowdfunding and investment of new business opportunities and creativities in exchange for products or investment income. Our birdie coin project represents the bridge connecting the traditional business model with decentralized technology.

Our community

Fundabird, powered by Creative Venture Group, establishes ourselves as industry-leading pioneers and a viable alternative source of crowdfunding. We combine our experience and expertise in financing, marketing, and engineering to make ideas happen, and dreams come true.

Our mission

Our mission is to lead crowdfunding with birdies, ideas to success, and explore the future. Everyone can become their own creator and investor. Fundabird brings full-service efficiency to crowdfunding with full commitment and passion.
